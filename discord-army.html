💡 How it works:

1. You tell Defy on Telegram what marketing campaign you want
2. Defy breaks it down and dispatches agents to specific channels
3. Each agent works in its channel, executing its specialized task
4. Results flow back to Defy, who reports to you
5. You oversee everything from Telegram - no manual Discord work!
